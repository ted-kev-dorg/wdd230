Abidjan Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose:

Abidjan Chamber of Commerce is an organization charged with representing the interests of commercial, industrial and service companies, training entrepreneurs and supporting businesses. Our mission is to support, inform and advise businesses at every stage of their development. More broadly, we contribute to the economic development of the region through our wide-ranging action plans.

Color Schema

Our selected color scheme reflects the chamber's professional and welcoming nature:

  • Primary Color (Navy Blue): Used for headings and the header background.
  • Accent Color (Gold): Used for accents, buttons, and links.

Typography

We have chosen the following fonts to ensure readability and a professional appearance:

  • Heading Font (Roboto): Used for all headings.
  • Body Font (Open Sans): Used for all body text.
